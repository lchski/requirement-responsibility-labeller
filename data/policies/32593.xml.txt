    <clauses><clause anchor="3.1.1">The Government of Canada has the necessary  assets and services in place to support program delivery to Canadians;</clause></clauses></clause><clause anchor="3.2">The expected results are that:

        <clauses><clause anchor="4.1.2.1.">Are  commensurate with the level of risk and complexity of the investment decisions;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.2.2.">Enable  effective life-cycle management; </clause><clause anchor="4.1.2.3.">Integrate  input and include an effective internal challenge function from the  department's project and programme management, procurement, real property,  materiel management, information technology, finance, human resource, and legal  functions; and</clause><clause anchor="4.1.2.4.">Are  fair, open and transparent;</clause></clauses></clause><clause anchor="4.1.3">Ensuring that investment decisions:
			<clauses><clause anchor="4.1.3.1.">Are  aligned with results as set out in the <em><a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/corporate/programs.html">Departmental  Results Framework</a></em>;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.2.">Consider  areas of greatest risk in achieving departmental objectives;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.3.">Are  documented and communicated across the department;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.4.">Are  informed by timely and accurate performance information;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.5.">Demonstrate  value for money and sound stewardship, taking into account the life-cycle costs  of assets and services;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.6.">Uphold  the obligations of the Crown with respect to Indigenous peoples;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.7.">Demonstrate  strategic consideration of market conditions and alternative and innovative approaches  to procurement; </clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.8.">Consider  the inputs from project management, procurement, real property, and materiel  management and other applicable internal and external stakeholders;</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.9.">Consider  opportunities to advance government socio-economic and environmental objectives,  such as accessibility and reducing greenhouse gas emissions; and</clause><clause anchor="4.1.3.10.">Leverage  opportunities for interdepartmental collaboration, including government-wide  portfolio solutions; </clause></clauses></clause><clause anchor="4.1.4">Ensuring that information is available to:
