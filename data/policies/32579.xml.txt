<?xml version="1.0" encoding="ISO-8859-1"?><doc title="Directive on the Business Number" documentID="32579" versionID="1" language="en" space="preserve" xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xsi:noNamespaceSchemaLocation="PolicyInstrumentSchema.HTML5.xsd"><chapters><chapter anchor="1" title="Effective date"><clauses><clause anchor="1.1">This directive takes effect on April 1, 2018.</clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="2" title="Authorities"><clauses><clause anchor="2.1">This directive is issued pursuant to section 3 of the Policy on Management of Information Technology.</clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="3" title="Objectives and expected results"><clauses><clause anchor="3.1">The objective of this directive is to ensure that departments and agencies use the Business Number (BN) as the standard identifier of businesses for all transactions between the businesses and the Government of Canada.</clause><clause anchor="3.2">The expected results of this directive are as follows:
				<clauses><clause anchor="3.2.1">Departments and agencies will use the BN as per this directive; and</clause><clause anchor="3.2.2">Departments and agencies will complete the requirements stipulated in section 4.1.1 of this directive no later than April 1, 2020.</clause></clauses></clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="4" title="Requirements"><clauses><clause anchor="4.1">The Chief Information Officer of departments and agencies is responsible for the following:
				<clauses><clause anchor="4.1.1">Ensuring the use of the BN by their department or agency as the standard identifier of businesses for all transactions between the businesses and their department or agency, by no later than April 1, 2020; and</clause><clause anchor="4.1.2">Ensuring the recognition by their department or agency of the Canada Revenue Agency as the Issuing Organization for the BN.</clause></clauses></clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="5" title="Roles of other government organizations"><clauses><clause anchor="5.1">This section identifies the roles of other key government organizations in relation to this directive. In and of itself, this section does not confer any authority.
				<clauses><clause anchor="5.1.1">Canada Revenue Agency is the designated Issuing Organization for the BN.</clause><clause anchor="5.1.2">Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada is the federal lead in promoting the use of the BN.</clause></clauses></clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="6" title="Application"><clauses><clause anchor="6.1">This directive applies to the organizations described in section 2 of the Policy on Management of Information Technology.</clause></clauses></chapter><chapter anchor="7" title="References"><clauses><clause
